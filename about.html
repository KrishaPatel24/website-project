ABOUT PAGE

The Winx all have different types of magic to fight evil and face their enimies.

Bloom

Bloom is the main character of this show. Her powers allow her to generate and control fire.

Stella

Stella is considered a princess on her home planet, Solario. Her magic allows her to control light and also make blasts of light energy.

Flora

With Flora's magic, she can control and create plants to do her bidding. She can also manipulate the Earth's energy.

Musa

Musa's magic allows her to control sound waves and she can create sonic blasts while controlling the sound around her.

Tecna

Tecna has technology related magic, she can control electricity and create sheilds of green energy.

Aisha

Aisha has the power to control any types of liquids, she can also create and manipulate a substance called Morphix which looks like pink jello.
